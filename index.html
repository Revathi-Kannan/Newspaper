In an official statement, he disclosed that the party has applied for registration with the Election Commission of India. Smith goes past Mark Waugh, only behind Ponting in elite catching list in Tests

NZ vs AUS: Steve Smith went past Mark Waugh’s tally of catches for Australia after taking a sharp slip catch to dismiss Will Young in the Wellington Test.

Steve Smith, on Saturday, went to second in the list of most catches by a fielder (non wicketkeeper) for Australia in Test cricket. Smith went past Mark Waugh’s tally of 181 during the opening Test against New Zealand at the Basin Reserve in Wellington. Steve got to the landmark after he took a sharp catch in the slip cordon to dismiss Will Young in the final session of Day 3.

Travis Head bowled one outside the off-stump and Young made a tentative push at the delivery.Earlier in the innings, Smith also took a decent catch to get rid of Kane Williamson. Nathan Lyon bowled from around the wicket, after which Williamson got a thick inside edge. Smith, standing at leg slip, showed great presence of mind to latch on to the ball.

Anant Ambani-Radhika Merchant Wedding: 14 New Temples To Be Built In Gujarat By Nita Mukesh Ambani Cultural Centre

The upcoming wedding of Anant Ambani and Radhika Merchant is one of the most anticipated events of 2024. With its pre-wedding celebrations just around the corner, the Ambani family has unveiled plans to construct 14 new temples in Jamnagar, Gujarat.

With the highly anticipated wedding of Anant Ambani and Radhika Merchant just around the corner, set for July 12, 2024, preparations are in full swing for their extravagant ceremony. Leading up to the main event, the pre-wedding celebrations are set to take place in the first week of March in Jamnagar, Gujarat. As the countdown to the pre-wedding festivities begins, Jamnagar is packed with activity, as the Nita Mukesh Ambani Cultural Centre has announced the construction of 14 temples in Jamnagar, Gujarat.

Featuring intricately carved pillars, sculptures of Gods and Goddesses, fresco-style paintings, and architecture inspired by generations of artistic heritage, the temple complex keeps India's rich cultural and spiritual identity at the heart of the wedding festivities.
